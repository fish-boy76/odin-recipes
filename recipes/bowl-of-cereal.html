Bowl of Cereal

Description

You open your eyes in a panic to the realization that you missed your morning alarm! You look at the clock on your nightstand to the terror that there is only barely enough time for breakfast after you get ready for work! If only there was some quick and easy breakfast food that could save your hungry growling stomach in the morning...

Oh wait... there is... a bowl of cereal. Let's make it!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cereal.
  • Milk.

Steps

  1. Take your cereal and pour it into a bowl.
  2. Pour the milk on top of the cereal until you have the desired amount of milk.

It's that easy! Phew... your hungry stomach is saved...

Bratwurst

Description

Have you ever thought to yourself "hotdogs are awesome but I wish they were somehow even awesomer..."? Me neither! But someone else did and now you're about to learn all about how to make the bratwurst!

Ingredients

  • Bratwurst sausage.
  • Hotdog bun (or any bun of your choice).
  • Sauerkraut.
  • Mustard.

Steps

  1. Put your bratwurst sausage on the grill.
  2. Let the sausage cook evenly on both sides, flip it every few minutes to check how each side is doing.
  3. Once the sausage appears to be cooked, take it off the grill and put it in your hotdog bun.
  4. Finally, top with the desired amount of sauerkraut and mustard.
